What is Oslo?

Chess training,
built for communities.

♟
200,000 real puzzles, always ready
Every puzzle comes from the Lichess Open Database — real game positions rated from beginner to insane. Easy, Medium, Hard, and Insane difficulty tiers calibrated to real ratings. Launch a puzzle in any channel with one command.
🎯
Train specific weaknesses
Filter by 10 tactical themes — sacrifice, fork, pin, endgame, mateIn1, and more. Stop drilling what you already know.
🏆
Compete with your server
Difficulty-weighted scoring, streaks, clean solve bonuses, and both server and global leaderboards.
⚡
Built for scale
Fully async architecture with PostgreSQL — multiple users solve simultaneously with zero lag or blocking.

Help Keep Oslo Alive

Keeping Oslo online 24/7 involves real monthly costs for cloud hosting, database servers, and real-time rendering. Every contribution goes directly toward these bills to keep the service growing. Your support means the world—thank you for keeping this project alive. ☕
